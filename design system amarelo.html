06 // Motion

Interaction Principles

Scroll Reveal .sys-reveal.sys-active

Initial Y offset + Blur -> Opacity 1. Duration 1.2s utilizing cubic-bezier(0.16, 1, 0.3, 1).

Stagger Delays .sys-delay-*

Sequential loading of elements increasing in 100ms intervals up to 500ms.

Hover Transformations transition-all

Components elevate (-4px Y) and exchange borders. Buttons adopt dramatic radial shadows (glow) to represent solid matter acting over the void.
